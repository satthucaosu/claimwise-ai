# Travel Insurance Policy

Document ID: POL-TRAVEL-001
Product: ClaimWise Standard Travel Protection
Effective date: 2026-01-01
Currency: EUR

## Coverage Overview

The ClaimWise Standard Travel Protection policy provides coverage for selected travel-related incidents that occur after the policyholder has purchased the policy and before the covered trip has ended. Covered incidents may include trip cancellation, travel delay, lost baggage, delayed baggage, and emergency medical expenses abroad.

Coverage applies only when the policyholder can provide evidence of the incident, proof of travel, and required supporting documents. The insurer may request additional information when the claim is incomplete, inconsistent, or outside standard automated approval rules.

This policy does not guarantee payment for every inconvenience during travel. Each claim must meet the eligibility rules, documentation requirements, reimbursement limits, and exclusions described in this document and in the general claim submission guidelines.

## Trip Cancellation

Trip cancellation coverage may reimburse non-refundable travel expenses when the policyholder cancels a trip before departure for a covered reason.

Covered cancellation reasons include:

- sudden illness or injury of the policyholder
- death or serious illness of an immediate family member
- severe weather that prevents departure
- official travel restriction issued after booking
- jury duty or legal summons that cannot be postponed

Required documents for trip cancellation:

- booking confirmation
- proof of payment
- cancellation confirmation from the travel provider
- evidence supporting the reason for cancellation, such as a medical certificate or official notice

Trip cancellation is not covered when the policyholder changes plans for personal preference, work schedule changes, lack of passport validity, or failure to obtain required visas.

## Travel Delay

Travel delay coverage applies when the departure or arrival of a scheduled transport service is delayed by more than 6 hours. A delay of exactly 6 hours or less is not covered under this policy.

Covered delay causes include:

- airline operational disruption
- severe weather
- transport strike
- airport closure
- mechanical issue confirmed by the carrier

The policy may reimburse reasonable meals, refreshments, and essential local transport purchased during the delay. Travel delay meals and refreshments are reimbursed up to 50 EUR per day. Hotel reimbursement may be considered only when an overnight delay is confirmed by the carrier.

Required documents for travel delay:

- original itinerary or booking confirmation
- boarding pass or proof of check-in
- written delay confirmation from the airline or transport provider
- receipts for claimed expenses

If the delay confirmation is missing, the claim must be routed to manual review. If the delay duration is unclear, the claim must also be routed to manual review.

## Lost or Delayed Baggage

Lost baggage coverage applies when checked baggage is permanently lost by the airline or transport provider. Delayed baggage coverage applies when checked baggage is returned late and the policyholder needs to purchase essential items.

Required documents for lost baggage:

- baggage tag
- airline irregularity report
- police report when theft or suspected theft is involved
- receipts for the lost items, or an itemized list if receipts are unavailable
- final written confirmation from the airline that the baggage is lost

Required documents for delayed baggage:

- baggage tag
- airline irregularity report
- proof of delivery time or baggage return confirmation
- receipts for essential replacement items

Lost baggage reimbursement is capped at 1,500 EUR per insured trip. High-value items such as jewelry, professional equipment, cash, and electronics may require additional review and may be subject to lower sub-limits.

## Medical Emergency Abroad

Emergency medical coverage applies to unexpected illness or injury during a covered trip outside the policyholder's home country. The treatment must be medically necessary and provided by a licensed medical provider.

Required documents for medical emergency claims:

- medical report
- invoice from the medical provider
- proof of payment
- travel itinerary
- prescription when medication costs are claimed

The policy does not cover elective treatment, cosmetic procedures, routine checkups, or treatment for a known condition that was excluded at policy purchase.

## Exclusions

The following situations are excluded unless explicitly approved in writing:

- incidents caused intentionally by the policyholder
- claims involving false or altered documents
- travel booked after a known disruption was announced
- expenses already reimbursed by an airline, hotel, employer, or another insurer
- losses caused by alcohol or drug misuse
- failure to take reasonable steps to reduce the loss
- missing documents after repeated requests

## Required Documents Summary

Travel delay claims require itinerary, boarding pass or check-in proof, carrier delay confirmation, and receipts.

Lost baggage claims require baggage tag, airline irregularity report, police report when theft is involved, receipts or itemized list, and airline confirmation of loss.

Trip cancellation claims require booking confirmation, proof of payment, cancellation confirmation, and evidence supporting the covered cancellation reason.

Medical emergency claims require medical report, provider invoice, proof of payment, itinerary, and prescription if medication is claimed.

## Manual Review Triggers

A travel claim must be manually reviewed when:

- delay confirmation is missing
- delay duration cannot be verified
- claimed amount exceeds the standard reimbursement cap
- documents appear inconsistent
- high-value baggage items are claimed without receipts
- the incident may involve an exclusion
